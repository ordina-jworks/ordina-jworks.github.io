Ordina JWorks career path

The Ordina JWorks career path defines several experience levels, from Novice to Master. Usually, companies only offer a training when you're a young graduate. We also want to give potential and existing colleagues the chance to level up during their career. We therefore setup two education programmes which an Ordina JWorks consultant can follow. One in the beginning of his or her career, and one for when someone already gained professional experience.
