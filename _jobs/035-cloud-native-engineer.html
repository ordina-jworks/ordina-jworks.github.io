Our Application Platforms practice aims to empower multidisciplinary teams with expertise such as cloud-native deployment, cloud platforms and CI/CD pipelines. We continuously evaluate new tools, technologies and methods to improve the speed, efficiency and scalability of cloud-native solutions.

As a Cloud-native engineer you are multi-tasked, you feel at home in a development or platform team. Within our agile development team you are jointly responsible for building features with business impact. In addition, you will strengthen the team with your expertise in automating the Software Delivery Lifecycle.

This translates among others into setting up CI/CD pipelines, application management via monitoring & logging and configuration of the cloud infrastructure. If you are active in a platform team, you will help outline the cloud infrastructure on Azure, AWS, Google and VMware Tanzu.

You will support multiple development teams through best practices, guidelines, tooling and support. You will carry out the DevOps values and culture! You build it, you run it!

    Who we are looking for

  • You have more than 2 years of experience in Cloud-Native Software Development.
  • Affinity with cloud-based provisioning, monitoring, troubleshooting and related cloud automation technologies is a must
  • You understand the pros and cons of cloud technologies
  • You have experience with CaaS, PaaS and Cloud specific SaaS technologies
  • You have experience with and insight into the Software Delivery Lifecycle
  • You have extensive experience with tools for containerization and orchestration such as Docker and Kubernetes
  • Opensource mindset and Linux experience is a plus.
  • You have experience with CI/CD automation tools such as Jenkins, Azure DevOps, AWS CodeBuild, Spinnaker, Concourse or Bamboo and artifact repositories such as Nexus or Artifactory
  • You have experience working in an agile environment
  • You can learn new technologies independently

What we offer

  • You contribute to challenging projects at top international and Belgian companies from various sectors
  • You work in a high-tech environment with a focus on knowledge and innovation, an open no-nonsense company culture with room for own initiative
  • You will be part of the most impressive Java-club in Belgium
  • You keep your technical and social skills up-to-date at our Ordina Academy and are supported in obtaining certificates
  • You receive a competitive salary package with additional benefits such as a company car, laptop, insurance package, meal vouchers, expense account, etc.
  • You can participate in fun business and team building activities
  • You can optimize your net salary thanks to our IP-reward
