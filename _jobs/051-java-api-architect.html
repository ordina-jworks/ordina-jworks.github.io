As API architect you are the bridge between business and IT, you know how to support organizations in the development of their API strategy. From your experience you know how to translate business questions into the most optimal IT solution. You do this by mapping the application landscape and reusable services of the customer. You always start from the service economy and API-first thinking. In addition, you have experience with integration projects and you know the different patterns that you can apply to turn an organization into a connected company. You have a vision on how to establish effective API governance within the organization resulting in a clear API approach and reference architecture. As a specialist you are perfectly aware of the importance of well designed APIs. You have enough technical experience and expertise as a technical architect to have teams of developers and analysts implement APIs correctly.

Who are we looking for?

  • You have 10 years of relevant work experience with at least 2 years as an application or solution architect
  • You have in-depth knowledge of RESTful API design, API-first development and API contracts such as OpenAPI, Pact or Spring Cloud Contract
  • You have a technical background in cloud-native development with good visibility into the Java landscape, containerization and orchestration, and cloud platforms such as Azure or AWS
  • You are familiar with development methodologies, standards, technical risks and architectural patterns including event-driven architectures, cloud-native architectures, n-tier, onion architecture, ...
  • You have experience with application modernization and integration patterns
  • Expertise in API gateway products such as Azure API Manager, Amazon API Gateway or other is a plus
  • You share your knowledge with your colleagues through our competence centers on topics such as architecture, API design, enterprise integration, cloud and security
  • You can communicate in Dutch and English, French is a plus

What we offer

  • You contribute to challenging projects at top international and Belgian companies in various sectors
  • You work in a high-tech environment with focus on knowledge and innovation , an open no-nonsense company culture with room for own initiative
  • You will be part of JWorks, a dynamic development unit within Ordina
  • Standing still is going backwards, you will be part of an architect team where knowledge is actively shared
  • You keep your technical and social skills up-to-date at our Ordina Academy and you will be supported in obtaining certificates
  • You get the opportunity to participate in our competence center workshops to keep your knowledge up-to-date or to expand it
  • You will receive a competitive salary package with additional benefits such as a company car, laptop, insurance package, meal vouchers, expense account etc.
  • You can participate in fun business and team building activities
  • You can optimize your net salary thanks to our IP-reward
