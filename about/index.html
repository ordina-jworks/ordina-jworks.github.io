About us

We want to help our customers excel in their domain by leveraging IT tech innovations in the Java & cloud ecosystem. We accomplish this with highly skilled teams of experts and strong leaders, that are drawing their expertise from our community. This way we will tackle any business challenge our customers might face by building the right digital products.

Backend Practice

Within our Backend practice we have two focus areas today:

  • We help our customers build customised solutions
  • We help them modernise their applications.
    • Tailor-made solutions are realised according to today's technological standards. A major focus today is the development of APIs. In application modernisation, we guide the client in their approach to legacy applications: typically, we do an audit of the existing application and then come up with a modernisation proposal. In both cases, we want to unburden the customer by also providing maintenance and support.

Application Platforms Practice

Our Cloud Engineers automate the Software Delivery Lifecycle. This translates, among other things, into setting up CI/CD pipelines, application management via monitoring and logging, and configuration of the cloud infrastructure.
